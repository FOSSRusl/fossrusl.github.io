some of our recently held events

Best Events

We have achieved a considerable sum of events which enlightened many learners of our university.
And we are looking forward to organize more events in the near future.

JUL | 19 2019

WebVR Workshop

WebVR workshop is a great opportunity for all the learners to access to new areas in the web development criteria. the event was filled with enjoyment and charisma and many chances were open for them who preferred the area of web development.

JUN | 11 2019

Mozilla BugHunter

Mozilla bughunter was all about debugging. But not only that it was an event that enlightened different types of people who aere related to IT and other Non-IT related personnels. There would be mkore events similar to this in the future.

NOV | 20 2018

Mozilla Outreach

Mozilla outreach was an event that literally taught the participants the shortest way to success there were various discussions on the extent each indivitual can reach within a limited time of period. We hoped of organizing more events like this in the future.

JUN | 03 2018

Mozilla sumo Sprint

Mozilla Sumo Sprint was all about saving the world from behind the comfort of the computer on the forums. The forum support an the social support was immense. Twitter was greatly helpful and also other social media networks. On suggestions another events would be conducted in this area.
